About Me

Moriamo A. Onabanjo is a passionate Christian author and cultural historian who weaves faith-based fiction with rich historical narratives. Born in Plainfield, New Jersey, and raised in Nigeria, she brings a unique perspective to her storytelling that bridges cultures and continents.

As an architect turned storyteller, Moriamo holds a degree in architecture and has channeled her expertise in sacred geometry and historical research into creating compelling tales. Her works in The African Mystery Series explore themes of prophecy, ancient mysteries, personal journeys, and the enduring power of faith.

Through her writing, Moriamo aims to inspire readers to discover the hidden connections between history, faith, and identity. Her stories often draw from Nigerian heritage, biblical history, and global mysteries, creating narratives that resonate with readers seeking both entertainment and spiritual insight.

When she's not writing, Moriamo enjoys researching historical artifacts, exploring architectural wonders, and sharing her passion for storytelling with audiences worldwide.
