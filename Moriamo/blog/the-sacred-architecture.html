The Sacred Architecture Behind "The Builder"

March 10, 2026

As an architect turned author, I drew heavily from my background in sacred geometry and architectural history when writing "The Builder." The novel explores the hidden blueprints of Solomon's Temple and the profound symbolism embedded in ancient structures.

The Temple's Divine Design

Solomon's Temple, as described in the Hebrew Bible, was more than just a building—it was a cosmic model of the universe. The inner sanctuary (Holy of Holies) represented the spiritual realm, while the outer courts symbolized the material world. Every dimension, every proportion held deep theological meaning.

Researching this topic revealed fascinating connections between biblical architecture and modern spiritual symbolism. The golden ratio, sacred geometry, and astronomical alignments all played roles in the temple's design—elements that continue to inspire my work today.

Sacred Geometry in Storytelling

When crafting the narrative, I imagined how ancient builders might have hidden messages within the temple's structure—blueprints that could only be read by those who understood the sacred language of numbers and proportions. This became a central theme in "The Builder."

The characters in my novel discover that the physical architecture mirrors a spiritual reality—that buildings can be conduits for divine communication. It's a concept that resonates throughout history, from the pyramids of Egypt to the cathedrals of medieval Europe.

Modern Applications

What I find most fascinating is how these ancient principles still influence modern architecture. From the layout of government buildings to the design of sacred spaces today, the echoes of Solomon's Temple remain. My hope is that readers will look at both ancient and modern structures with new eyes after reading "The Builder."

The intersection of faith, history, and architecture creates a rich tapestry that I continue to explore in all my writing. The Builder is just the beginning of this journey through sacred spaces and their hidden meanings.
